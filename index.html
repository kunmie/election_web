Eligibility to contest as a candidate

To be eligible to contest, prospective contestants should have met the following requirements before registration;

  • You must posses a membership card of a registtered political party.
  • You must have won the primary election of the party you belong to.
  • You must have no history of criminal dealings.
  • You must have no pending court cases.
  • see section 31(d) of the 1999 constitution of the federal repulic of nigeria for further details.

Having fufiled all requirement and confirmed eligibility Register here.

Eligibilty and Voting requirements

To be eligible to vote, prospective voters should have met the following requirements before registration;

  • You must posses a permanent voters card.
  • You must be 18 years of age or above as the time of registration.
  • You must also have a valid proof of your Nigerian citizenship.
